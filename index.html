ABOUT ME

My name is leva Sukyte, I was born and raised in a small comunity in Kursenai town. I was a scout half of my life, and I devoted the other half to the pedapogical education of young people. I have learned a lot from young people - they are innovative, creative, they love what attracts attention, follows trends. It is important for young people to feel important, to stand out, to be unique! Based on this, I set my goal - to be able to draw attention and engage, follow and creatively and playfully perform the tasks assigned to me.
